Hearthlily — 晴萱

Private Membership Senior Concierge

An ultra-premium, design-led, tech-enabled private membership club for independent elders in Taipei, delivering absolute peace of mind and visual connection to global families.

👤 Founder / Lead: Félicia (UI/UX & Branding)
📍 Location: Taipei City, Taiwan
💳 Target Client: Affluent Native & Expat Families
⚙️ Tech Support: Morgan (Systems & Infra)

1. Executive Summary & Positioning

Hearthlily (晴萱) operates on an **ultra-premium, low-volume, high-margin subscription model**. Instead of commodity hourly chores, we sell **absolute reassurance, dignity, and visual family connection**.

By capping clients at **8–10 active families** for the solo founder, the business guarantees top-tier curation, zero burnout, and excellent margins. The service is built specifically for overworked Taipei corporate leaders or members of the Taiwanese diaspora in North America/Europe who need a trusted peer-level liaison to manage their parent's digital security, lifestyle, and health.

"When you can't be there, we can. Stay close, even from afar."

2. Market & Timing

  • A Super-Aged Reality: Taiwan is a super-aged society. Taipei City has the highest concentration in the nation: 24.18% of residents are 65+ (nearly 1 in 4).
  • The Private Wealth Market: High-net-worth senior households in districts like Da'an and Xinyi possess massive capital but face technological exclusion, digital security threats, and social isolation.
  • Félicia's Moat: Visual storytelling, professional design-led updates, and digital safety coaching. The brand's tech value is anchored by the **Hearth Score™** and the secure **Family Hub** portal.

4. Premium Operations & Risk Mitigation

  • Premium Transport Policy: Seniors are **never transported on scooters**. All transit utilizes pre-booked premium taxis or VTCs, billed directly to the client's credit card on file as a pass-through expense.
  • Zero Cash Handling: Staff never handle cash, checkbooks, or bank credentials. All transactions are handled digitally via NewebPay or ECPay.
  • High-Limit Liability Insurance: Active professional liability and third-party accident coverage up to **NT$5,000,000** limit.
  • Founder Bottleneck Mitigation: Codifying all operations (visit SOPs, reporting templates, tone-of-voice guide, escalation matrices) in the **Hearthlily Operating Manual** to prepare for scaling in Year 2.

## 1. The Insight

Aging in Asia’s wealthiest families is no longer just a care problem.  
It has become a **trust, visibility, and emotional connection** problem.

Adult children — often living abroad or working at the highest levels — carry constant low-grade anxiety about their parents. They don’t need another caregiver. They need someone they can **trust completely** to be their eyes, ears, and calm presence on the ground.

The current market offers only two extremes:  
- Low-touch domestic help  
- High-dependency medical care  

There is almost nothing in between for families who want **dignity + intelligence + discretion**.

---

## 2. The Solution

**Hearthlily** is building a new category:  
**Private Family Office for Aging**

We act as the family’s trusted local representative — a discreet, highly capable, design-minded partner who manages the non-medical reality of aging with elegance and rigor.

We combine:
- High-touch human presence
- Professional observation and early detection
- Ethical digital safety coaching
- Beautiful, structured family reporting
- Crisis readiness and coordination

Into one seamless, premium service.

**Tagline**  
*When you can’t be there, we can.*

## 3. Why Now

Taiwan is super-aged. Taipei has one of the highest concentrations of 65+ residents in Asia.

At the same time:
- Family structures have shrunk dramatically
- Wealth has increased
- Adult children are more geographically dispersed than ever
- Digital scams targeting seniors are rising fast

The families who feel this gap most acutely are precisely those with the means to pay for a superior solution.

Executive Summary

Hearthlily is an ultra-premium, design-led Private Family Office for Aging based in Taipei. We do not sell caregiving hours or domestic help. We sell **absolute peace of mind, dignity, and visual connection** to affluent families — particularly those with members living abroad.

By acting as a trusted, high-touch local representative, we help families manage the aging of their parents with clarity, confidence, and elegance.

Core Model

  • Limited to a maximum of 12 families (scarcity as a feature).
  • High-touch, subscription-based membership.
  • Strong emphasis on **design intelligence** (beautiful reporting) and **ethical technology** (co-learning rather than surveillance).
  • Founder-led in Year 1 for maximum quality and relationship depth.

Mission: Preserve independence, dignity, and intergenerational connection during aging — through human trust, intelligent systems, and beautiful communication.

The Problem

Taiwan is one of Asia’s fastest-aging societies. Taipei has one of the highest concentrations of residents aged 65+ in the country. Several structural trends create a painful gap for affluent families:

  • Shrinking family support — Many parents now have only one child, often living far away.
  • Geographic separation — Adult children in the US, Europe, Singapore, or Japan experience constant guilt and anxiety.
  • Digital vulnerability — Seniors are increasingly targeted by sophisticated scams (LINE, banking, impersonation).
  • Emotional isolation — Loneliness accelerates cognitive and physical decline.
  • Information asymmetry — Families often discover problems too late because they lack reliable, nuanced visibility.

Current Inadequate Solutions

  • Domestic helpers — Good for chores, poor at judgment and reporting.
  • Traditional care agencies — Transactional and focused on dependency.
  • Doing it remotely — Leads to burnout, guilt, and anxiety for the children.

The market gap is clear: There is no premium service that combines high emotional intelligence, design quality, and operational rigor between basic caregiving and a full family office.

Our Solution: Private Family Office for Aging

Hearthlily becomes the family’s trusted local representative — a discreet, intelligent, and aesthetically refined partner. We integrate five core pillars into one seamless service:

Digital Safety: Co-Learning Model (Ethical Core)

We have rejected intrusive monitoring models. Instead, we practice Digital Literacy & Scam Defense Co-Learning:

  • We work with the senior (not on them).
  • Joint configuration of tools (e.g. Whoscall, large contrast keypads).
  • Review of suspicious messages together.
  • Role-playing exercises to build confidence.
  • The senior remains in control and feels respected.

Progress is documented positively in the UX Family Reports (e.g., *“Mrs. Lin independently identified and declined a suspicious message this month”*). This approach is ethically sound, legally safer, and actually deepens trust.

Operations & Risk Management

  • Transport: Only premium taxis or chauffeured vehicles (never scooters). Billed as pass-through.
  • Cash & Credentials: Strict zero-handling policy. No access to banking pin codes or credit cards.
  • Insurance: Professional liability + accident coverage (minimum NT$5–10M limits).
  • Founder Protection: All processes documented in the Hearthlily Operating Manual (SOPs, tone of voice, escalation matrices) — ready for future team members.

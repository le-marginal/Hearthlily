# Hearthlily — 晴萱
> **Premium Senior Lifestyle Concierge**
> Taipei, Taiwan · 2026

Hearthlily (晴萱) is a premium, non-medical senior lifestyle concierge service in Taipei, Taiwan. It bridges the gap between commodity companion services and heavy clinical care by offering highly personalized companionship, medical accompaniment, digital safety training, and design-led home reorganization.

This repository serves as the shared anchor point for the Hearthlily project, synchronized between **Morgan's Antigravity 2.0** (technical lead & infrastructure) and **Félicia's Antigravity 2.0** (founder, concierge, & design lead).

## 🛠️ Service Offerings

1.  **À-la-carte Hourly Concierge (NT$700/h)**
    *   Medical accompaniment & post-consultation summaries.
    *   Digital safety coaching & scam prevention curriculum.
    *   Administrative paperwork & subsidy navigation.
    *   *Policy:* 4-hour minimum for one-off bookings (flexible micro-visits for subscribers).
2.  **Monthly "Peace of Mind" Retainers**
    *   **Silver (NT$10,000/mo):** ~3 visits/month + standard report.
    *   **Gold (NT$18,000/mo):** ~6 visits/month + priority booking + signature **UX Family Report**.
3.  **Life Transition & Spatial Reorganization (NT$25,000–50,000)**
    *   Ergonomic, fall-proof, design-led home structuring for downsizing or aging-in-place.

---

## 🔒 Operational Constraints & Safeguards

*   **Premium Transport:** No senior transportation on scooters. All travel during sessions uses pre-booked taxis/VTC, billed as a pass-through cost directly to the client's card.
*   **Legal Onboarding:** Standard three-part agreement (Non-medical co-responsibility, Emergency 119 protocol, Financial boundary policy preventing cash handling).
*   **Billing:** Errands/reimbursements and retainers run through local digital rails (LINE Pay / ECPay / NewebPay).

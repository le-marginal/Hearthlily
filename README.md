> **Private Family Office for Aging**

## 🔒 Operational Constraints & Safeguards

*   **Premium Transport:** No senior transportation on scooters. All travel during sessions uses pre-booked premium taxis or chauffeured vehicles, billed as a pass-through cost directly to the client.
*   **Legal Onboarding:** Standard three-part agreement (Non-medical co-responsibility, Emergency 119 protocol, Financial boundary policy preventing cash/credentials handling).
*   **Billing:** Retainers and packages run through local digital rails (LINE Pay / ECPay / NewebPay).
*   **Co-Learning Digital Safety:** We practice a collaborative co-learning model (working *with* the senior to audit LINE messages, configuring phone contrast/font settings, and teaching scam detection), strictly rejecting spyware or hidden monitoring.

The Market Opportunity

Taipei's Super-Aged Reality

A Rapidly Aging Demographic:

  • Taiwan became a super-aged society in late 2025.
  • Taipei City has the highest concentration in the country: 24.18% of residents are 65+ (nearly 1 in 4).
  • Birth rates hit record lows for a tenth straight year, accelerating family care stress.

The Gap in Public Care (LTC 3.0):

  • Strictly means-tested and target-focused on severe disability.
  • Active, independent "young-old" seniors do not qualify.
  • Families exhaust quotas quickly and must self-pay.

We occupy the premium middle ground, offering lifestyle care instead of medical sitting.

Risk Mitigation

Safety, Transport & Trust Rules

Premium Transit Policy:

  • Seniors are never transported on scooters.
  • All transit during client hours uses taxis or premium VTC, billed directly to the client as a pass-through cost.
  • Félicia's scooter is strictly for solo admin errands.

Onboarding & Finance Safeguards:

  • Zero Cash Handling: Staff never touch cash/passwords. All payments and reimbursement run via digital billing (LINE Pay, ECPay, or NewebPay).
  • 3-Document Signoff: Non-medical co-responsibility, emergency hospital authorization, and financial boundary sheets.

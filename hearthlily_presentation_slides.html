Slide 1 · The Insight

The Core Family Problem

A Care vs. Connection Problem:

  • Aging in Asia’s wealthiest families is no longer just a physical care issue. It is a **trust, visibility, and emotional connection** challenge.
  • Adult children working globally carry constant low-grade anxiety. They do not want raw utility care; they need an elegant local representative on the ground.

The Current Market Gap:

  • Extreme A (Low-end): Domestic helpers good at basic chores but poor at judgment, safety mapping, and design-led updates.
  • Extreme B (Clinical): High-dependency medical sits focused on end-of-life pathology.

We target the high-end gap: Dignity, Intelligence, and Discretion.

Private Family Office for Aging

1. Human Presence

3. Family Visibility

Slide 3 · Why Now

Demographic Catalyst

Taipei's Super-Aged Reality:

  • Taiwan is super-aged. **Taipei City has the highest concentration of 65+ residents (24.18%)**.
  • Shrinking family structures: many elders have only one child, often residing overseas (US, EU, Singapore).

Vulnerability & Asset Protection:

  • Seniors are primary targets for online phishing, LINE impersonations, and financial scams.
  • Affluent families have substantial capital but limited time to manage routine security.

Those with the means to pay are precisely those feeling the pain most acutely.

Slide 7 · Curation Process

Hearth Score™ & Value Loop

The Hearth Score™ Index:

Every curated companion visit generates structured feedback across 4 core dimensions on the Family Hub portal:

  • Physical Safety: Home safety, mobility, hazards.
  • Vitality: Cognition, sleep, nutrition, mood.
  • Connection: Excursions, family calls, social.
  • Digital Security: Scam defense, accounts.

Structured Insight

Turns qualitative observations into clear, metrics-driven data, giving overseas children immediate clarity on how their parents are doing without looking clinical.
